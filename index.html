How To BUY

Step 1: Set up a wallet

Choose a compatible Ethereum wallet like MetaMask, Trust Wallet, or Coinbase Wallet. Set up and secure your wallet before proceeding. 

Step 2: Get Ethereum (ETH)

Buy Ethereum from popular exchanges like Coinbase, Binance, or Kraken and transfer it to your wallet.

Step 3: Access ape.store

Go to ape.store, connect your Ethereum wallet, and search for the $PEPEI token using the contract address.

Step 4: Swap ETH for $PEPEI

Enter the amount of ETH you want to swap for $PEPEI , review the transaction details, and confirm the transaction. Wait for the transaction to be processed and check your wallet for the purchased $PEPEI tokens.
